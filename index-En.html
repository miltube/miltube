Quality Control

In-house laboratory
certified core strength

Every single production batch undergoes rigorous laboratory checks before leaving our plant. We test mechanical load limits, exact dimensional tolerances, and optimal moisture content — ensuring failure-free winding on your conversion lines.

Radial Crush Resistance

Measures core resistance against gripping forces applied during high-tension winding. Vital for high-speed converters.

Axial Compression Test

Validates load capacity for vertical storage and stacking. Prevents core deformation during warehousing.

Dimensional Stability

Cores are thermally conditioned and measured for straightness (max 1.5 mm/m) to counter ambient humidity shifts.

Moisture Management

Maintained strictly between 8–10% for optimal structural core integrity. Monitored via calibrated digital hygrometers.
